## OSI model (7 layers)

### 6 — Presentation (encoding, encryption)

### 5 — Session (connection management)

### 4 — Transport (TCP, UDP)

### 3 — Network (IP, routing)

### 2 — Data Link (Ethernet, MAC)

### 1 — Physical (cables, signals)
