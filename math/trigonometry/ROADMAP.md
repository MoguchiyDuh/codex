# Trigonometry — Course Roadmap

---

## Phases

### Phase 1 — Foundations
Topics: right triangle definitions (sin, cos, tan, csc, sec, cot),
unit circle, radian vs degree conversion, exact values (30/45/60/90).

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 2 — Graphs & Transformations
Topics: graphs of sin, cos, tan, amplitude, period, phase shift, vertical shift,
`A·sin(Bx + C) + D` form, inverse trig functions and their domains.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 3 — Trigonometric Identities
Topics: Pythagorean identities, sum/difference formulas, double angle, half angle,
product-to-sum, sum-to-product, identity proofs.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 4 — Solving Equations & Triangles
Topics: trig equations (general solutions), sine rule, cosine rule,
area formula (`½ab·sinC`), ambiguous case (SSA).

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 5 — Applications
Topics: vectors via trig, polar coordinates, parametric equations,
harmonic motion, small angle approximation.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

## Completion

All 5 phases passed (grade ≥ C).
