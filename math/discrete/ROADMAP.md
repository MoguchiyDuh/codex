# Discrete Mathematics — Course Roadmap

Foundation for algorithms, cryptography, and formal reasoning.

---

## Phases

### Phase 1 — Logic & Proofs
Topics: propositional logic, logical connectives, truth tables, tautologies,
predicate logic, quantifiers, proof techniques (direct, contrapositive, contradiction,
induction, strong induction).

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 2 — Sets, Relations, Functions
Topics: set operations, power set, Cartesian product, relations (reflexive,
symmetric, transitive, equivalence), functions (injective, surjective, bijective),
composition, inverse.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 3 — Combinatorics
Topics: multiplication and addition principles, permutations, combinations,
binomial theorem, pigeonhole principle, inclusion-exclusion.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 4 — Probability
Topics: sample space, events, conditional probability, Bayes' theorem,
independence, discrete distributions (Bernoulli, binomial, geometric),
expected value, variance.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 5 — Graph Theory
Topics: graph definitions (directed, undirected, weighted), degree, paths,
cycles, connectivity, trees, spanning trees, bipartite graphs, planarity,
graph coloring, Euler and Hamiltonian paths.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 6 — Number Theory
Topics: divisibility, GCD (Euclidean algorithm), modular arithmetic,
Fermat's little theorem, Euler's theorem, RSA basics, primality.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

## Completion

All 6 phases passed (grade ≥ C).
