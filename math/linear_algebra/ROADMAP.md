# Linear Algebra — Course Roadmap

Computational and geometric intuition in parallel.

---

## Phases

### Phase 1 — Vectors
Topics: vector operations (add, scale, dot product, cross product),
geometric interpretation, magnitude, unit vectors, angle between vectors,
projection.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 2 — Matrices & Operations
Topics: matrix addition/multiplication, transpose, identity, zero matrix,
matrix-vector product as linear transformation, non-commutativity of multiplication.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 3 — Systems of Linear Equations
Topics: Gaussian elimination, row echelon form, reduced row echelon form,
back substitution, consistency (no solution, unique, infinite), rank.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 4 — Determinants & Inverses
Topics: 2×2 and 3×3 determinants, cofactor expansion, properties of determinants,
matrix inverse existence, adjugate method, Cramer's rule.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 5 — Vector Spaces
Topics: vector space axioms, subspaces, span, linear independence,
basis, dimension, null space, column space, rank-nullity theorem.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 6 — Linear Transformations
Topics: definition, kernel, image, matrix representation,
change of basis, composition as matrix multiplication.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 7 — Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors
Topics: characteristic polynomial, eigenspace, diagonalization,
symmetric matrices, geometric vs algebraic multiplicity,
PCA intuition, Markov chains.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

## Completion

All 7 phases passed (grade ≥ C).
