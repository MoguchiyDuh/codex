# Algebra — Course Roadmap

---

## Phases

### Phase 1 — Fundamentals & Polynomials
Topics: algebraic identities, polynomial operations, factoring,
rational expressions, partial fractions.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 2 — Equations & Inequalities
Topics: linear, quadratic (completing the square, quadratic formula, discriminant),
polynomial equations, rational inequalities, absolute value equations.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 3 — Functions
Topics: domain, codomain, range, composition, inverse, even/odd,
piecewise functions, transformation (shift, scale, reflect).

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 4 — Exponentials & Logarithms
Topics: exponential functions, natural base `e`, logarithm rules,
change of base, solving exponential and log equations, growth/decay models.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 5 — Sequences & Series
Topics: arithmetic and geometric progressions, sum formulas,
binomial theorem, infinite geometric series convergence.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 6 — Complex Numbers
Topics: `a + bi` form, arithmetic, modulus, argument, polar form,
De Moivre's theorem, roots of complex numbers.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

## Completion

All 6 phases passed (grade ≥ C).
