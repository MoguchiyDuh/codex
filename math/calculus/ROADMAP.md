# Calculus — Course Roadmap

Single-variable first, then multivariable. Rigorous definitions, not just rules.

---

## Phases

### Phase 1 — Limits & Continuity
Topics: epsilon-delta definition, limit laws, one-sided limits,
limits at infinity, indeterminate forms, continuity, intermediate value theorem.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 2 — Derivatives
Topics: definition as limit, differentiation rules (power, product, quotient, chain),
implicit differentiation, higher-order derivatives, logarithmic differentiation.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 3 — Applications of Derivatives
Topics: tangent lines, related rates, linearization, L'Hôpital's rule,
critical points, first/second derivative tests, concavity, optimization,
mean value theorem.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 4 — Integrals
Topics: antiderivatives, definite vs indefinite integrals,
fundamental theorem of calculus (both parts), substitution, area under a curve.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 5 — Integration Techniques
Topics: integration by parts, trigonometric integrals, trig substitution,
partial fractions, improper integrals, convergence.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 6 — Sequences & Series
Topics: sequence convergence, series convergence tests (ratio, root, comparison,
integral, alternating series), power series, Taylor and Maclaurin series,
radius of convergence.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

### Phase 7 — Multivariable Calculus
Topics: partial derivatives, gradient, directional derivative, chain rule,
critical points of `f(x,y)`, double integrals, Fubini's theorem, change of variables.

**Exam status:** not taken
**Grade:** —

---

## Completion

All 7 phases passed (grade ≥ C).
